Usage
-----

Now you don't need a Kinect to start development on your project. Install our <a href="http://kinesis.io/download" target="_blank">Kinesis Simulator (http://kinesis.io/download)</a> and you are on your way.

1. Click on the ___Start Server___ button.

2. Click on the ___Connect Kinect___ button.

3. Refresh the browser window.

### Gesture simulation ###

#### Original Position (Bounds)
  Starting co-ordinates of a gesture. You can set the starting x,y and z co-ordinates and click on any gesture. This will simulate the clicked gesture with starting position as x, y, z. 

![Alt Kinesis Simulator Gestures](/images/kinesis/KS-gestures.png)
  
### Hand Tracking ###
![Alt Kinesis Simulator Hand Tracking](/images/kinesis/KS-hand-tracking.png)

#### The Red Ball
  This red ball simulates the hand movement. It provides the x and y co-ordinates of the hand.
  
#### Depth
  This slider adds a z co-ordinate to the hand simulation via red ball.<br /><br /> To use it, just set a particular depth and move the red ball to send the new depth position along with position co-ordinates
